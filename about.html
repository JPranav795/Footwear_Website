Our Story

LuxeFeet was founded in 2010 by Anna Smith, a visionary with a passion for redefining luxury footwear. What began as a small atelier in Milan has grown into a global brand, celebrated for its exquisite craftsmanship and timeless designs. Over the past 15 years, LuxeFeet has adorned the feet of royalty, celebrities, and discerning individuals who seek elegance without compromise.

Our journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of perfection, blending traditional techniques with innovative materials. From our first boutique in Paris to our flagship stores in New York, Tokyo, and Dubai, LuxeFeet continues to set the standard for sophistication in footwear.

Our Craftsmanship

At LuxeFeet, every shoe is a masterpiece, handcrafted by artisans with decades of experience. Our process begins with selecting premium Italian leather, suede, and exotic materials, followed by meticulous stitching and finishing. Each pair undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure perfection.

Our workshops in Florence are a blend of tradition and innovation, where master craftsmen use time-honored techniques alongside cutting-edge technology. This dedication to artistry ensures that every LuxeFeet shoe is not just footwear, but a legacy of elegance.
